# The Dawn of Yield-Native Finance

The financial world stands at an irreversible inflection point. The legacy systems, defined by fragmented stablecoins, low-yield neobanks, and costly remittance corridors, are fundamentally incompatible with the demands of the digital economy. They fail to harness the power of tokenized assets, compliant yield, and instant global settlement.

Zoth is not merely an improvement on existing models; it is the definitive architecture for the next era of finance.
