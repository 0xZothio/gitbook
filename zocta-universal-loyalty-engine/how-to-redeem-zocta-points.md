# How to Redeem Zocta Points

Zocta Points are not redeemable before maturity. After maturity, and subject to program rules, Zocta Points may be used across Zoth ecosystem reward categories.

| **Category** | **Description**                                                                                                       |
| ------------ | --------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| CLAIM        | Redeem Zocta Points for $ZOTH tokens after TGE                                                                        |
| ACCESS       | Unlock discount coupons and exclusive experiences, including curated events, concerts, and private experiences        |
| COLLECT      | Redeem points for Zoth merchandise and co-branded drops, including wearables, collectibles, and limited-edition items |
