# Where to Check Your Points

Users can view their Zocta Points from the Zocta Campaign dashboard. The dashboard shows total points, LP earnings, referral earnings, referral link, season countdown, campaign cap, and referee activity.

Users can track:

* Your Zocta Balance
* LP Earnings
* Referral Earnings
* TVL Cap
* Season Countdown
* Referral Link
* Referee Activity
* Total TVL across Referees
