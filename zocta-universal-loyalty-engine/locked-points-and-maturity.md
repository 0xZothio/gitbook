# Locked Points and Maturity

Zocta Points appear as Locked Points during the campaign. Locked Points are accumulated points that are not yet redeemable.

Points become eligible for redemption only after maturity and according to the applicable campaign rules. Zocta Points are non-transferable, accrue daily during eligible participation, and are not redeemable before maturity.

Liquidity must be maintained for the required campaign period. Early withdrawal may result in forfeited rewards.
