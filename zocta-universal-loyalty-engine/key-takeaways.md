# Key Takeaways

* Zocta Points are Zoth’s ecosystem rewards layer.
* The zOPAL Zocta Campaign is the first live Zocta activation.
* Users can collect points through LP Activity and Referral Activity.
* Referral points are awarded only when referred users become active LPs.
* Points accrue daily and appear as Locked Points during the campaign.
* Season 1 has a 180-day timeline and a $10M TVL cap.
* Zocta Points are non-transferable and not redeemable before maturity.
* Future Zocta campaigns may introduce additional ways to collect points.

One ecosystem. Many ways to move. One rewards layer connecting it all.
