# How to Collect Zocta Points?

* **LP Activity**: earn points by maintaining eligible capital in zOPAL.
* **Referral Activity**: earn points when referred users become active LPs.
* **Future Campaigns**: additional eligible activities may be introduced through official Zoth programs.

Users can collect Zocta Points through eligible activities announced by Zoth. The first live activation is the zOPAL Zocta Campaign, where points are earned through LP Activity and Referral Activity.
