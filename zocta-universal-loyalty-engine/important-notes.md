# Important Notes

* Zocta Points are non-monetary by design.
* Zocta Points are non-transferable.
* Points accrue daily during eligible campaign participation.
* Points appear as Locked Points before maturity.
* Points are not redeemable before maturity.
* Referral joins alone do not earn points.
* Referral points begin when the referred user becomes an active LP.
* Early withdrawal may result in forfeited rewards.
* Each campaign may have its own TVL cap, timeline, rates, and rules
